Where to stay

3 April 2019 ☕️ 1 min read

Arriving on Saturday or Sunday

We haven’t arranged any specific deals for those arriving on Saturday/Sunday. This is because the deals we had initially organised were more expensive than the rates we found on websites like Booking.com.

For those who are arriving on Saturday/Sunday there are a number of options for you to choose from. If you would like to stay in Basel and visit the city, we can recommend a number of hotels in the centre of town. It is then only a 15 minute drive (20CHF by taxi) from the centre to the wedding hall (Reithalle Wenkenhof).

Basel

If you are arriving on the Sunday and want a location closer to the wedding hall, there are a number of hotels we can recommend. The wedding is taking place at the Reithalle Wenkenhof, which is on the border of Switzerland and Germany, therefore there are a number of places to choose from on both sides of the border in Riehen (Switzerland) and Lörrach (Germany).

Riehen

Riehen is a 10 minute walk to the venue and has one hotel we can recommend.

  • Landgasthof

Lörrach

Lörrach is a 15 minute drive to the venue, across the border from Germany. For those staying in Lörrach, please bring identification when crossing the border, just in case there is a police stop (very rare).

  • Hotel Meyerhof ⭐️⭐️⭐️ (with airport shuttle)
  • Bar-Drei König Lörrach ⭐️⭐️⭐️ (with airport shuttle)
  • Hotel Villa Elben ⭐️⭐️⭐️

Airbnb

There are a decent number of Airbnbs scattered throughout Basel, Lörrach and Riehen. They can be considerably cheaper than hotels but they do get booked quite far in advance. Forward planning is required!
